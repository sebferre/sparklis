Example queries for Sparklis

This page lists a number of queries over different endpoints (mostly DBpedia) that can be built in Sparklis. For each example query, a link enables to directly jump to it in Sparklis, and to navigate further.

Example queries demonstrated in the YouTube demo

  1. List of films directed by Tim Burton and starring somebody born after 1980, ordered by increasing birth date. Open in Sparklis
    give me every film
    • whose director is Tim Burton
    • and whose starring has the lowest-to-highest birth date after 1980
  2. List of film writers born in France with their English name, an optional depiction, their number of films as a writer (in decreasing order), and the list of their known birth dates. Open in Sparklis
    • give me every writer
      • that is the writer of a film
      • and whose label has a language matching en
      • and that optionally has a depiction
      • and that has a birth date
      • and that has any birth place whose country is France
    • and for each result
      • give me the highest-to-lowest number of film
    • and for each result
      • give me the list of birth date

Example queries about Nobel Prizes

  1. List of Peace Nobel prize laureates, in decreasing year. Open in Sparklis
    give me every laureate whose nobel prize
    • has as a category Peace
    • and has the highest-to-lowest year
  2. List of Nobel prize laureates, by decreasing number of Nobel prizes. Open in Sparklis
    • give me every laureate that has a nobel prize
    • and for each laureate
      • give me the highest-to-lowest number of nobel prize
  3. Number of Nobel prize laureates by gender. Open in Sparklis
    • give me every laureate that has a gender
    • and for each gender
      • give me the number of laureate

Example queries about geographical facts in the Mondial database

The Open in Sparklis links below include parameters to use English labels for classes and properties, and to use a Mondial-specific labelling property for entities. You may have to click the button after following the links to see the proper labels because they are retrieved asynchronously. Questions 7-10 illustrates the new capabilites of Sparklis to express computation expressions, nested aggregations, filtering expressions, and their combinations.

  • Selections

    1. Find rivers taking their source in Russia, and flowing into a lake. Open in Sparklis
      give me every river
      • whose source is located in Russia
      • and that flows into a lake
    2. Which capitals of which countries are on the border to the Atlantic Ocean? Open in Sparklis
      give me every city
      • that is the capital of a country
      • and located at Atlantic Ocean
    3. Give French cities with a population higher than 200,000 located along a river. Open in Sparklis
      give me every city
      • that is the city of France
      • and whose population is higher or equal to 200000
      • and located at a river
    4. Which languages are spoken in Spain? In which proportions? Open in Sparklis
      give me every spoken language
      • whose country is Spain
      • and that has a language
      • and that has the highest-to-lowest percentage

  • Basic aggregations

    1. How many countries are there in Europe? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every country whose continent is Europe
      • and give me the number of country
    2. What is the average city population in China? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every city
        • that is the city of China
        • and that has a population
      • and give me the average population

  • Simple aggregations (or cubes)

    1. What is the population of continents, given the population of countries? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every country
        • that has a continent
        • and that has a population
      • and for each continent
        • give me the total population
    2. How many countries are there for each continent, and for each language spoken by at least 10% of the people? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every country
        • that has a continent
        • and that is the country of a spoken language
          • that has a language
          • and whose percentage is higher or equal to 10
      • and for
        • each continent
        • and each language
        • give me the number of country

  • Multiple aggregations

    1. Give me the average population and the average area of countries, for each continent. Open in Sparklis
      • give me every country
        • that has a continent
        • and that has a population
        • and that has an area
      • and for each continent
        • give me the average population
      • and for each continent
        • give me the average area
    2. Give me the number of European seaside city, and their average population, for each country, and for each sea. Open in Sparklis
      • give me every city
        • that is the city of a country whose continent is Europe
        • and whose location near the body of water is a sea
        • and that has a population
      • and for
        • each country
        • and each location near the body of water
        • give me the number of city
      • and for
        • each country
        • and each location near the body of water
        • give me the average population

  • Nested aggregations

    1. What is the average area of archipelagos, given that the area is only known for individual islands? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every archipelago that is the archipelago of an island that has an area
      • and for each archipelago
        • give me the total area
      • and give me the average total area
    2. What is the distribution of the number of islands in archipelagos, i.e. the number of archipelagos for each number of islands per archipelago. Open in Sparklis
      • give me every archipelago that is the archipelago of an island
      • and for each archipelago
        • give me the number of island
      • and for the lowest-to-highest number of island
        • give me the number of archipelago

  • Expressions in bindings and filters

    1. Give me the population density for each country, from population and area. Open in Sparklis
      • give me every country
        • that has a population
        • and that has an area
      • and give me the population / the area
    2. Which countries have a service GDP less than the agricultural and industrial GDP combined, and have an agricultural GDP higher than the industrial GDP? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every country
        • that has an agricultural GDP
        • and that has a gdp ind
        • and that has a service GDP
      • and where the service GDP < the agricultural GDP + the gdp ind and the agricultural GDP > the gdp ind

  • Complex combinations

    1. Give me countries whose GDP per capita (=total GDP*1e6/population) is above the average. Open in Sparklis
      • give me every country
        • that has a total GDP
        • and that has a population
      • and give me GDP per capita = the total GDP * 1e6 / the population
      • and give me the average GDP per capita
      • and where the GDP per capita > the average GDP per capita
    2. Has Russia more rivers longer than 1000km than USA? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every river
        • whose length is higher or equal to 1000
        • and whose location is a country
      • and for the location Russia
        • give me the number of river
      • and for the location United States
        • give me the number of river
      • and where the 1st number of river > the 2nd number of river
    3. Which proportion of cities are near a body of water, for each country? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every city
        • that is the city of a country
        • and that optionally has a location near the body of water
      • and give me near water ? = 1 or 0 whether the location near the body of water is bound
      • and for
        • each country
        • and the near water ? 1
        • give me the number of city
      • and for each country
        • give me the number of city
      • and give me the 1st number of city / the 2nd number of city

  • Ranking and top-K questions

    1. List countries by decreasing population density (=population/area). Open in Sparklis
      • give me every country
        • that has a population
        • and that has an area
      • and give me highest-to-lowest the population / the area
    2. Which mountain range has the highest number of volcanos? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every mountains that is the in mountains of a volcano
      • and for each mountains
        • give me the highest-to-lowest number of volcano
    3. Which are the 3 most common numbers of islands in archipelagos? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every archipelago that is the archipelago of an island
      • and for each archipelago
        • give me the number of island
      • and for each number of island
        • give me the highest-to-lowest number of archipelago
    4. Which religions are present in which countries at more than 5 percent? How many such countries are there for each religion, in decreasing order? Open in Sparklis
      • give me every belief
        • that has a religion
        • and that has a country
        • and whose percentage is higher or equal to 5
      • and for each religion
        • give me the highest-to-lowest number of country
